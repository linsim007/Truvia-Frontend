Lab testing for the way you live.

Truvian is disrupting the blood testing industry by developing an automated, FDA-cleared benchtop diagnostic system to provide lab-accurate results in 20 minutes for a comprehensive suite of health tests, from a small sample of blood. Our proprietary approach fulfills the promise of delivering accessible and affordable blood testing from one small blood sample, in minutes, in a retail setting or private clinic.

Benchtop system. Benchmark results.

Powered by patented technologies and intelligent integration, Truvian’s compact instrument delivers a convenient and affordable alternative to off-site traditional labs, providing lab-accurate rapid insights to inform healthcare decisions. The groundbreaking diagnostic platform combines clinical chemistries, immunoassays and hematology in one instrument, in a single run.

  • Lab-accurate results for a full suite of health tests using a single, small sample of blood produced onsite within minutes and delivered to patients.
  • Unique fusion of three crucial modalities delivers comprehensive results through a single run, at a depth unachievable with competitive technologies.
  • Compact and easy to run, using novel reagent technology requiring no refrigeration.

No single on-site diagnostic platform, until now, covers such a wide range of analytes while providing accuracy that rivals offsite labs. And by combining three crucial modalities in a single instrument, the system eliminates the need to send out samples and wait days or weeks for results.

7 drops of blood, 1 comprehensive panel.

Truvian’s comprehensive wellness panel covers 40 of the most commonly ordered diagnostic tests, including basic and advanced nutrients, complete blood count, thyroid and blood sugar, cholesterol and liver and kidney levels.

Insights in minutes, on-site.

Fast, accurate results are delivered electronically to the healthcare provider through a secure, easy-to-use digital platform. Get actionable results in minutes. Gain insights that can last a lifetime.

About us

Truvian Sciences was founded in 2015 by individuals with deep domain experience in healthcare, diagnostics and consumer technology. Today, the team has expanded to include top scientists, engineers, and healthcare business leaders – each bringing specialized expertise to drive Truvian’s mission. Together, we are disrupting the blood testing industry to fulfill an unmet need, developing an automated benchtop FDA-cleared system that allows practitioners to deliver data to patients instantaneously on a secure and easy-to-use mobile platform.
